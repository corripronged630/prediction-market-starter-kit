## 🖥️ What this app does

Prediction Market Starter Kit is a desktop web app that helps you browse prediction markets, place trades, and manage your positions in one place. It uses a simple sign-in flow, so you do not need to install browser extensions to get started.

Use it to:

- Search for markets
- View price charts
- Buy and sell outcome shares
- Check open and closed positions
- Review your trade history

## 🔎 Main features

### 📊 Browse and search markets

Scroll through the market feed and search for events by name or topic. The app shows market data and price history so you can compare outcomes.

### 💱 Trade outcome shares

Buy or sell shares through the market order book. The app connects to Polymarket tools to help you place trades.

### 🪪 Easy sign-in

Sign in with email or a wallet. The app uses embedded wallets, so you do not need extra browser extensions.

### 🪙 Gasless setup

The app creates and configures a wallet in the background. This helps new users begin with less setup.

### 📁 Portfolio view

See your current positions, closed positions, and trade history in one place.

## 🧭 How to use it

### 1. Sign in

Open the app and sign in with email or wallet. Follow the on-screen prompts.

### 2. Find a market

Use search or scroll through the market list. Open a market page to see details and price movement.

### 3. Place a trade

Choose the outcome you want to buy or sell. Review the order and confirm it.

### 4. Check your positions

Open the portfolio area to see active trades, past trades, and gains or losses.

### 5. Manage your activity

Return to the market page anytime to change your position based on new information.

## 🗂️ Project layout

The app is split into a few main parts:

- `app/` — pages and API routes
- `components/` — screen parts used across the app
- `hooks/` — trading and balance helpers
- `lib/polymarket/` — market client, relayer, and session data
- `lib/` — market data, chart data, and price tools
